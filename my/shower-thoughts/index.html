      • In Chess, what’s the primary goal for each piece? Naturally, the ultimate goal is to ensure your king’s survival while aiming to checkmate the opponent’s king. But what to do in the meantime? You advance your pawns forward; their objective is to reach the opponent’s fortress and loot it up to ascend to the rank of queen or minister. On the flip side, the opponent’s pieces strive to resist and ensure their own pawns have the same opportunity.
