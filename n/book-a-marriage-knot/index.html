'A Marriage Knot' By Shobana Mahadevan - Book Notes

7 min

Thoughts#

  • Feel good book
  • Classic love story, uses common tropes
    • aunt is bad, uncle is good but helpless, mother-in-law is good and supportive
    • the female lead gets saved by a prince (in terms of both qualities and riches) and she is looking for basic respect and freedom - classic damsel in distress
    • the male lead gets pushed by the female lead and he acknowledges his responsibilities and finds his strength, he gets challenged by the female’s thought process and in turn keeps falling for her
    • both complete each other, opposites attract (if both complement each other), which is the case here
  • No strong conflict: Both (female and male leads) lack any negative trait, the resolution of their conflict is merely a conversation away - what they agreed upon and what they want now
